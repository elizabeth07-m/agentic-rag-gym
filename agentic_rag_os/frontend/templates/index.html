Process

From raw data to RL training signals
in 4 steps

01

Ingest your data

Upload text files to your RAG project. The system chunks, embeds, and indexes them into a private FAISS knowledge base — free tier up to 5 MB.

POST /api/v1/projects/{'{'}id{'}'}/documents
Content-Type: multipart/form-data
02

Define reward rules

Compose reward functions from 7 built-in rule types — keywords, formats, citations, reasoning patterns, length constraints, and custom regex.

{"{"}"type": "reasoning_steps", "weight": 1.5{"}"}
{"{"}"type": "citation_present", "weight": 2.0{"}"}
03

Compute reward batch

Send up to 500 prompt/response pairs per call. Get back per-rule breakdowns, aggregate scores, and algorithm-normalized rewards (GRPO/PPO/DPO).

{"{"}"reward": 0.847,
 "breakdown": {"{"}"citations": 0.99, "reasoning": 0.71{"}"}{"}"}
04

Export & fine-tune

Export as JSON, CSV, or HuggingFace dataset format. Plug directly into your GRPO / PPO training loop. Ship better models, faster.

GET /api/v1/rewards/{'{'}id{'}'}/computations/{'{'}cid{'}'}/export
?format=hf_dataset
